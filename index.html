June Race Reports & Headlines

Verstappen wins in Spain after intense Norris duel

Max Verstappen fended off Lando Norris to claim victory at the Barcelona GP, while Hamilton returned to the podium — his first of the season.

Russell dominates Montreal chaos

George Russell emerged victorious at the Canadian GP, which saw a dramatic safety car, wet start, and Russell’s first win of the season.

Groundhog disrupts Hamilton’s car

Lewis Hamilton's race in Canada was compromised after colliding with a groundhog costing him half a second per lap.

Haas & Norris incidents stir drama

McLaren’s Pascal Piastri benefitted from teammates colliding, while Haas and other drivers faced chaos during the Canadian GP.

June 2024 Recap

  • June 9 – Canadian GP: Rain-wet conditions, Russell takes lead, Verstappen engineered a daring win.
  • June 23 – Spanish GP: A fierce duel sees Verstappen hold Norris at bay before Hamilton resurfaces on podium.
  • June 30 – Austrian GP: Russell claimed victory following a late collision between Verstappen and Norris.
